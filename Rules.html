Rules:

Objective: To have the most points at the end of the game.

You draw cards of 7 different colours (6 if you are playing with three or two players ) and at the end of the game you score the three colors of which you have the most cards according to the following table:

1 card: 1 point
2 cards: 3 points
3 cards: 6 points
4 cards: 10 points
5 cards: 15 points
6 cards: 21 points
7 or more cards: 21 points
The colours that are not among the top three colours discount you points according also to the previous table. For example if you have:
  • 2 green cards
  • 2 light blue cards
  • 4 red cards
  • 5 orange cards
  • 5 yellow cards
  • 6 violet cards
  • 7 brown cards
You would score:
+ 21 (7 brown cards)
+ 21 (6 violet cards)
+ 15 (5 yellow cards)
- 15 (5 orange cards)
- 10 (4 red cards)
- 3 (2 light blue cards)
- 3 (2 green cards)
= 26 points

Rainbow cards count as any colour, and "+2" cards simmply add points and don't count towards any colour.

Turn development:

On your turn you have two options:

  • Draw a card from the deck and place it next to any row (there are as many rows as players playing)
  • Choose a row and take all cards from that row. That row is unavailable until the end of the current round.
  • You can't place cards on a row that has 3 cards already.
  • When all rows are full (i.e., have 3 cards on it), your only option is to take a row. When all rows are taken, the round ends.
  • Each round (except for the first one) starts with the last player to play in the previous round.
